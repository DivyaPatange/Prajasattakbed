About US

Indian Youth and Women's development society was formed and registered at Nagpur under the leadership of Shri. Prakash Gajbhiye Prajasattak college of education started by society in the year 2006. Our college is a college of numerous opportunities. The environment of our college is designed to maximize learning through purposeful activity. We seek to develope our students physically, mentally, emotionally keeping in mind the rich Indian Cultural heritage. Our college is co-educational admit students from all over Maharashtra and other parts of country.
